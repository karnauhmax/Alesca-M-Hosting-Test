  • ALESCA ORION

    Farm management IT system

    Alesca Orion is a web and smartphone-based farm operations and supply chain management IT system. The platform schedules all daily tasks for farm operators, tracks all activities and production cycle details, organizes supplier and customer information, and provides insights through data dashboards.

  • ALESCA SPROUT GEN 2

    Monitoring & automation control box

    Alesca Sprout is a real-time, remote environmental monitoring and equipment automation Internet-of-Things device for indoor and greenhouse farm operations. This sensor-based control box automates lighting, irrigation, nutrient dosing, and other farm electrical equipment.

  • ALESCA VISIO

    Plant health & farm monitoring system

    Alesca Visio is an AI multi-spectral camera capable of non- invasive yield and plant health monitoring for farm operations and plant research. This AIoT device is equipped with specialized optical bandpass filters to capture, process, and analyze photosynthetic data to provide actional insights to farmers in near-real time.

  • ALESCA SPECTRA

    High efficiency lighting solutions

    Alesca Spectra is a high efficiency, full-spectrum LED module for indoor vertical farming. The low wattage LED provides the optimal ratio of light wavelengths to maximize photosynthetic activity, plant health, and produce quality while ensuring operator safety and ease of installation and maintenance.
