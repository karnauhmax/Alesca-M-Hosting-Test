Alesca Life is an agricultural technology company that builds indoor vertical farms and farm management solutions to make food production more localized and data-driven. Alesca was named a World Economic Forum Technology Pioneer as one of the global industry leaders and was selected into prestigious global accelerator programs including Stanford StartX, Barclays Capital Unreasonable Impact, and Dubai Futures.

The Quality Control & Assurance Engineer is a highly visible function that will play a key role in developing remote monitoring and autonomous control systems to ensure Alesca’s global farm facilities operate efficiently and consistently. The position requires a strong understanding of fundamental engineering principles and the ability to work with embedded software and hardware subsystems. This role will also be involved in developing Alesca’s longer-term manufacturing and tech strategies.

About You

Our team is growing, and while there’s plenty of room for all kinds of personalities and skill sets to succeed, there are certain qualities that will help you thrive at Alesca including an incredible work ethic, strong collaborative mindset, relentless drive to push past your limits and try new things. We’re looking for someone with the energy, dedication, and confidence to take on big challenges, make mistakes fast and early, embrace constructive feedback, then recover quickly with fresh, creative solutions. We value a fearless willingness to defend great work and a strong passion to revolutionize the $20 trillion global agricultural supply chain. If this sounds like you, read on and let’s connect soon.

What You’ll Do

  • Lead technical team on the design, development, and debugging of application software for embedded Linux devices and bare metal using C/C++
  • Develop software that communicates with other devices through a variety of network and RF protocols
  • Evaluate and improve efficiency, stability, and scalability of system resources
  • Debug base level hardware interfaces (USB, UART, PWM, GPIO, I2C, etc)
  • Develop and debug MySQL DB, MQTT protocol, RTOS and Linux drivers, and device tree
  • Support firmware update testing and global OTA deployments
  • Develop and run software unit tests and participate actively in peer reviews
  • Research, evaluate, and actively incorporate new, best-in-class technologies, tools and processes
  • Work effectively with the engineering team and guide/mentor junior software and hardware engineers

Skills You’ll Need

  • BS degree in Computer Science or Engineering/Technical discipline with 5+ years of development experience
  • Extensive experience managing internal software engineering team and project managing external development partners and vendors
  • Strong C/C++ programming skills in embedded systems and extensive experience with multi-threading
  • Hands on experience with development and troubleshooting of embedded systems
  • Strong understanding of communication protocols including SPI, I2C, UART, etc.
  • Extensive experience with various microprocessors/architectures including ARM, RISC, MIPS, etc.
  • Extensive prior experience in documenting and presenting engineering designs, proposals, and reports to other engineering and leadership team members
  • Ability to read schematics and data sheets for components
  • Ability to work effectively as part of a team as well as independently
  • Bilingual in spoken and written Chinese (Mandarin) and English

Other Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

  • Experience and interest in writing code to test IO devices and/or functional units
  • Experience with Linux driver, IoT device development, ability to design electrical control circuits is a plus
  • Experience developing OTA (over the air programming) is a plus
  • Competence in one or more languages such as Assembly, Python or JavaScript is a plus
  • Strong documentation and verbal communications skills. Disciplined self-starter
  • Familiar with various collaboration tools, MS Project, PowerPoint, Word, Excel, and Outlook
